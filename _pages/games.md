## Spherica

---

> Top down shooter | PVP
{% include googleDrivePlayer.html id="1k2HKXvHv_zJU81vHFgJBCCi-qavciwqx" %}

<p>Spherica is a team-based pvp top down shooter game. Each team killing each other for points, competing who got the higher points before the timer runs out.</p>
<p>The game take place on top of a spherical world, this means the bullet you shoot out will travel across the map and eventually returns back to you!</p>
<p>Each characters have different shooting styles and a special ability.</p>
<p>The map contains objects that helps you gain an advantage over your opponents:  </p>
- Pets - The pets will spawn on a fixed interval at a fixed location. Approach them to be friends with them and they will provide you buffs to overtake your opponents!
- Water puddle - Player can stay inside the puddle to stay hidden from the enemies, setting up for a sneak attack or escape from them!

## Exploswitch

---

> Action | PVP
{% include googleDrivePlayer.html id="1jmTmEUeRbvq5NQjHB7LeIlNraNeiKKPN" %}

<p>Exploswitch is a 2 players pvp action game.</p>
<p>Players throw bombs at each other to try and knock them out to the spikes at the border.</p>
<p>The core game mechanic is inspired by Dota 2's Morphling. Player can transfer their attribute points from Stregth to Agility and vice versa.</p>
- Stregth: Affect the knockback distance of your bomb.
- Agility: Affect your movement speed.

## Elemental Lord

---

> Strategy | Puzzle | PVP
{% include googleDrivePlayer.html id="1nK7j5epsOuGGMRQIE5Y9ZKOxQqpYjpJ7" %}

<p>Elemental Lord is a 2 players pvp strategy/puzzle game.</p>
<p>There are various characters with unique abilities, each character fall into a role:</p>
- Tanker
- Attacker
- Supporter
<p>Each player will pick a character from each role to make up their team.</p>
<p>After the match start, players will take turns matching symbols at the top to trigger characters' abilities. Each type of symbol corresponds to a role: </p>
- Shield: Tanker
- Sword: Attacker
- Magic Staff: Supporter
<p>Each character have 3 abilities, it is triggered depending on how many symbols you matched. Example for the character Lunar: </p>
- Match 1 symbol: Grant shields to allies
- Match 2 symbols: Deal damage to an enemy
- Match 3 symbols: Heal allies
<p>Matched symbols are destroyed and new symbols will fall down to fill in the empty spaces.</p>
