Games

These are the projects I have been involved in.

Spherica

Top down shooter | PVP

Spherica is a team-based pvp top down shooter game. Each team killing each other for points, competing who got the higher points before the timer runs out.

The game take place on top of a spherical world, this means the bullet you shoot out will travel across the map and eventually returns back to you!

Each characters have different shooting styles and a special ability.

The map contains objects that helps you gain an advantage over your opponents:

  • Pets - The pets will spawn on a fixed interval at a fixed location. Approach them to be friends with them and they will provide you buffs to overtake your opponents!
  • Water puddle - Player can stay inside the puddle to stay hidden from the enemies, setting up for a sneak attack or escape from them!



Together

Horror survival | Puzzles

Together is a horror survival game.

The story start with a young man trying to find his girlfriend inside an abandoned hospital. In the process of figuring out where is his girlfriend and why would she end up in an abandoned hospital, he encountered various paranormal incidents trying to stop him from reaching his goal.

The player have to solve puzzles along the way while avoiding the mysterious beings.



Exploswitch

Action | PVP

Exploswitch is a 2 players pvp action game.

Players throw bombs at each other to try and knock them out to the spikes at the border.

The core game mechanic is inspired by Dota 2's Morphling. Player can transfer their attribute points from Stregth to Agility and vice versa.

  • Stregth: Affect the knockback distance of your bomb.
  • Agility: Affect your movement speed.



Elemental Lord

Strategy | Puzzle | PVP

Elemental Lord is a 2 players pvp strategy/puzzle game.

There are various characters with unique abilities, each character fall into a role:

  • Tanker
  • Attacker
  • Supporter

Each player will pick a character from each role to make up their team.

After the match start, players will take turns matching symbols at the top to trigger characters' abilities. Each type of symbol corresponds to a role:

  • Shield: Tanker
  • Sword: Attacker
  • Magic Staff: Supporter

Each character have 3 abilities, it is triggered depending on how many symbols you matched. Example for the character Lunar:

  • Match 1 symbol: Grant shields to allies
  • Match 2 symbols: Deal damage to an enemy
  • Match 3 symbols: Heal allies

Matched symbols are destroyed and new symbols will fall down to fill in the empty spaces.
